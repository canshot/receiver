Calling All Students!

Cake Health is unveiling our most recent update, Cake Health 2.1, which gives students and faculty on college campuses around the country FREE access to all Cake Health services! We recognize the stress that comes with being a college student, and what better way to help you handle that stress than with our proven health-tracking services? Whether you're a first-year student learning to navigate being on your own, or you're in your last semester before stepping out into the world, Cake Health is here to help! Joining Cake Health through your university is easy; simply find us on your Apple or Android app store, download the app completely free of charge, and register your account. In order to link your app with our services to receive your FREE subscription, we just need some basic information, such as:

-Your university and graduating year

-Your full name and date of birth

-Your preferred email address and phone number

Once you provide this information, we can confirm your subscription through the app, and you're good to go! From there, you can track your insurance and healthcare documents and plans, as well as share information from your preferred health and fitness apps straight to our software in order to better tailor our services to you! Keeping track of your fitness has never been so easy! We look forward to helping you live your best college life.

Questions? Comments? Concerns? Be sure to email us at services@cakehealth.edu, and include your name, phone number, and university.
