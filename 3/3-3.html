Living off campus could help to save a lot of money. However, finding an off-campus apartment as a college student can be difficult. If you don’t have a substantial income, many landlords won’t approve you for a lease. Luckily, there are ways to make it work.

Here are 5 tips for you to renti an off-campus apartment with little to no income:

1. Consider a private home rather than a complex

2. Ask a friend or family member to cosign the lease

3. Pay more upfront

4. Find a roommate

5. Sublet an apartment
