Indeed's job search functionality allows you to tailor your job search to exactly what you want to do. Simply upload your resume to a free account on Indeed and you can begin your search any time, anywhere. In the "where" box of our job search, you can narrow your search down, not only to a city like Denver, but to a single zip code, whether it's yours or somewhere you plan to move.

You can also narrow down by requirements and qualifications, using job titles, experience levels, or search terms like "research". Once you know what area and type of job you're looking for, you can even set up job alerts to send you emails when new jobs meeting your search criteria are posted.
