UNC Charlotte implements digital content policy


User4 ibm_security@gmail.com

10:00 am Tuesday


Hi Student,

UNC Charlotte, has taken a decision to implement a strict policy on campus that would prevent the downloading of illegal copy of digital contents from the internet. A very famous and effective IBM Watsom System will be used for the mentioned policy. 

After analyzing high number of illegal downloads and the risks associated with them, the University has taken a decision to implement the above mentioned policy and thus support anti piracy.

The implementation of the policy requies every student enrolled in the UNC Charlotte to click on the below link and select his/her major in the drop down menu.

https://uncc/digital_policy/registration

Please be aware that no other information apart from your major is needed at the monet.
