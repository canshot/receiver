Plans for a new Hotel and Conference Center in UNC Charlotte!


User7 newsletter.uncc@gmail.com

10:27 am Monday


Hi Amit,

Hope you're having a great week! 

Here's the news update on the newly funded Hotel and Conference Center that's being built right now.

Where is it going to be?
The 4.4-acre site targeted for the project is located at the J.W. Clay Boulevard-UNCC stop on the Blue Line Extension light-rail line, located at the intersection of North Tryon Street. 

Why this project?
The project can be used as a major way of attracting tourists to Charlotte, trying to attract to the region, to University City and the campus, organizations that typically don’t come to Charlotte now because we don’t have a campus-based hotel.

When is it anticipated for completion?
The project is slated for completion for the 2020 batch of students.
