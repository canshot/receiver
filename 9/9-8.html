URGENT-- Review Payment for Hotel Funding


User8 uncc.students.newsletter@gmail.com

02:12 pm Monday


Hi Amit, 

This email is to inform you about the changes in the fee structure for the upcoming semesters.
 

UNC Charlotte, as a community has laid out a plan for a hotel, conference center on campus. It is planned to have it located at the J.W. Clay Boulevard-UNCC stop on the Blue Line Extension light-rail line, located at the intersection of North Tryon Street. It is an $84 Million funded project, which is built for all the students to use. 

As a requirement for room for conferences and to accomodate the visitors on campus, students are required to pay for their usage.
A new amount has been added to your 49er Billing Account, that will be a part of the fees for the upcoming semesters.

In order for you to exclude yourself from the service and revoke your contribution for the funding, please click here to sign into your account and review your billing details.

We thank you for your cooperation.
