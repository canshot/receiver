5 Ways To Get Off-Campus Housing Without Income


User6 info@zillow.com

12:41 pm Wednesday


Here are 5 steps to help you afford an apartment off-campus:

1. Don't rent from big complexes. Big apartment complexes often cost more.

2. Have a family member cosign. Cosigning with a family member with income is a good way to make sure there is a back up incase you cannot afford rent.

3. Have a roommate. Multiple room apartments are usually cheaper per room, so having a roommate to split the price can help bring down the amount you have to pay per month.

4. Pay more upfront. Paying more upfornt is a good way to get a better deal on a apartment.

5. Sublet an apartment. Subletting can bring in a little income while still being able to live within your own apartment.
